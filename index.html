Length Diversity

Explores the impact of input text length on motion generation using the prompt “the person crouches and walks forward.” (ground truth length: 196 frames).

  • MMM: Sensitive to length variations; misses crouching, struggles with order.
  • MoMask: Lacks walking and crouching; limited in motion diversity.
